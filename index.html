Galveston, after the crowds thin out.

There is a particular kind of magic along the island at the edge of the day: the air cools, the lights come on, and familiar places start to feel newly discovered.

This is the spirit of Out There: a closer look at the people, places, and local possibilities that can turn an ordinary afternoon into somewhere worth going.
